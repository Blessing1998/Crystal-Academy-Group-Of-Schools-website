THE BUSINESS IDEA

Zimbabwe has fairly large mumber of school going children who are at the moment facing difficulties in finding good schools which offer excellent educational service at affordable fees.During the hyperinflation and covid era,many schools were affected by teacher strikes and this led to poor provision of educational services.This caused an uproar and exorbitant fees which were charged placing parents on the harder side of the rock. Many private schools sprouted and most of them was run by unqualified teachers.There was an outcry by parents which fell on deaf ears as the gorvenment could not meet all the educational demands of most parents due to the unstabilized economy.lt is a well known fact that the most vulnerable children are those in high density locations such as Hobhouse.This scenario prompted lsaiah Musara together with his wife Thelma Musara to come up with Crystal Academy Group Of Schools in an attempt to meet the educational demands of their community and country at large.
